Pandemic Trends in Publishing

The pandemic introduced changes in the world of publishing. With more free time, people began to read more. This project sought to objectively identify some of those changes. Data was used from a business-to-business publishing sales and marketing platform called Edelweiss in order to make these assessments. Pre-pandemic years analyzed were 2018-2020 and pandemic years were 2021-2022.

Some of the key changes were that the number of fiction titles in certain categories like action, adventure, short stories, and crime jumped considerably. While technology also saw a substantial increase in titles published. Book prices averaged around a 10% increase, before the pandemic the year to year average was around 7%. And bloggers have grown considerably over the past few years. While all other user types have seen moderate but steady growth.
